Work Experience

  • 2022: Master’s Academic Project; Goa University

    • Conducted Phenological Studies of Trees in Socorro Plateau, Goa.  The project involved studying the phenological patterns of various trees in Socorro plateau, by plotting their flowering, fruiting and leafing growth patterns and also understanding the various inter-connections which exist in between the different flora of the region.

  • 2022: Internship; project run by Institute of Botany, Czech Academy of Sciences

    • Assisted in the sampling of plant material from high elevations of Western Himalayas for belowground and aboveground trait measurements, soil sampling and processing of collected samples. The research work was conducted as part of a Horizon 2020 project entitled “Functional differentiation in response to environmental variability in two contrasting shrub line species of high-altitude Himalaya.”
